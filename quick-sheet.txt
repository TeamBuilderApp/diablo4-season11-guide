D4 SEASON 11 – 1-PAGE QUICK SHEET

1) PRE-LAUNCH
- Finish campaign once, unlock mount, claim renown.
- Pick your Season 11 class + leveling build before login.

2) DAY 1 LEVELING
- Skip campaign.
- Do the Season 11 quest until the seasonal mechanic unlocks.
- Levels 1–15: high-density dungeon spam, upgrade weapon.
- Levels 15–50: Legions + Helltides + your best layout dungeons.

3) WORLD TIERS
- WT3 capstone ~40–50:
  - One strong damage aspect, basic defenses, some mobility.
- WT4 capstone ~60:
  - Sacred weapon, 2–3 stacked damage buckets (e.g. Vulnerable + Crit + Core),
  - Real damage reduction and a panic button.

4) ENDGAME LOOP (WT4)
- Rotate:
  - Nightmare Dungeons (XP + glyphs),
  - Helltides (mats + gear),
  - Seasonal activity,
  - Uber bosses (uniques/Ubers).

5) STAT PRIORITY
- Leveling: weapon DPS > everything else.
- Endgame:
  - Pick 2–3 buckets (e.g., Vulnerable + Crit + Core/DoT) and stack them.
- Always maintain:
  - 1 mobility skill,
  - 1 panic/defensive cooldown,
  - Solid armor/resists.

6) SAFE STARTER BUILDS (ADJUST FOR SEASON 11 PATCH)
- Necro: Bone Spear.
- Rogue: Twisting Blades.
- Sorc: Ball Lightning / Chain Lightning.
- Druid: Pulverize / Tornado.
- Barb: Bleed / Rend.

7) PLAYSTYLE RECOMMENDER
- Solo casual: Necro / Druid.
- Solo high skill: Rogue / Sorc.
- Group: Shout Barb + AoE carry (Sorc/Druid).
- Hardcore: Tank Druid, Bleed Barb, Minion Necro.

Use this sheet at Season 11 launch, for clan friends, or for alt planning.
